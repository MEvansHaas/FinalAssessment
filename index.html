Luther

Came into our family June 17, 1994
Died July 10,2013

This is a fun tribute page to our loyal companions!

Luther was a little puppy when he was left in my husband's truck on Martin Luther King day. He grew up with our children and was a member of our family for over 19 years

  1. He loved cheese and rides in the truck.
  2. He loved a good belly rub and early morning walks down our country road and chasing rabbits.
  3. He hated thunderstorms.
